Shipping Information

At Bodega Sur, we strive to provide you with seamless shipping. Please find below the details regarding our shipping policies for customers within the European Union.

Shipping destinations

We currently ship to all countries within the European Union.

Shipping methods

Shipping costs are calculated according to the weight of the order and the destination country, according to the prices of the operator. The total shipping cost will be communicated prior to the payment of the order.

  • Standard Shipping: Delivery within 4-6 business days

    • InPost: delivery within 1-2 business days

  • International shipping within the EU

    • DPD: delivery within 4-5 business days

    • Inpost: delivery within 4-5 business days

Order processing time

Orders are normally processed within 1-2 business days. You will receive a confirmation email or whatsapp message (depending on your communication preference) once your order has been dispatched.

Tracking your order

Once your order is shipped, you will receive an email with a tracking number, allowing you to monitor the status of your shipment.

Order charges

Prices with respect to shipping will be additionally charged to the total order amount.

Any additional custom duties and taxes are to be paid by the buyer.

Contact us

If you have any questions about shipping or your order, feel free to contact our customer service team at hola@bodegasur.com
